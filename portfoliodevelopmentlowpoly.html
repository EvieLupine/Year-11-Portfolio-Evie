Development

This is the development progress of the head for my low-poly character. I created this by adding a cube as a starting point for making my head and used tools to further extend, shape and warm the 3D shape until it resembled a head.

Testing

Once I warped the 3D shape till it resembled the front and side view of the head, I started editing and testing how it looked at other angles untill eventually the head was just how I wanted. Additionally I created more detail to the muzzle, teeth and back of the head.
