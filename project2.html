Project 2

Overview

My team is making a Text Adventure about surviving a fictional pandemic and the goal is to find cure for the virus to heal yourself. In the beginning you start as slightly infected and you have a limited amount of turns to find the cure before the virus takes over. The cure is in random location and guarded by the government, the inspiration of the virus is the COVID-19 pandemic. To find the cure you have to uncover a special area where the cure is guarenteed to be but a big bad guy guards it. In this area you are not likely to survive so you haveto be smart about your next move. In addition you also have to find things to defend yourself against threats, its self defense so its ethical in order to get the cure and survive, otherwise you die from getting sick.

End Users

Teenagers from New Zealand who like adventure and story games.
