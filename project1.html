Low Poly Character

Overview---→

I will make character that has an idle and running animation to present the way it will act. I want to make a character that is lupine because like most things in my work it has an element of wolf-ness that is unique to me and public.

View My Project

End Users

My target audience are children from ages of 9-14 who are wanting to play a game with asimple and nostalgic feel-good to it. This means the game must be innocent, cute and fun to play. To make it enganging the characters will have bubbly and pretty animations to continue focus. Mostly girls will be the appeal to the game and characters. There will be a blend of cute/pretty and powerful.

Requirements

  • The character must have four limbs and a face of such to identify it as.
  • The character must be able to make flexible and fluid movements to make it unique and playable.
  • The character is of either male, female or a non affirming gender to create diversity.
  • The character will have themes that raise awareness for disabilities.
  • The character is appropriate for children and brings no concerning themes.

Implications

Aesthetics

Setting a variety of colours to create an ongoing theme that relates to a feel good Aesthetic.

Functionality

Adding links to work and things that are easy to navigate.

Usability

Making the text and font accessible to people who have trouble reading and seeing. And links to social medias containing my artwork.
